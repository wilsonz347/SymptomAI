    About SymptomAI

    Empowering better health decisions through technology

    SymptomAI uses our cutting-edge machine learning model to provide a preliminary diagnosis based on your symptoms. Our website is designed to help you make informed decisions about seeking medical attention, based on personalized data.

    Built on a foundation of existing real-world data, our neural network considers a plethora of symptom combinations and patterns to create an initial assessment.

    We believe everyone deserves accessible health information. While Symptom AI is a powerful tool, it is designed to complement rather than replace professional medical care.
